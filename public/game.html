HOW TO PLAY
COMBAT
Play cards to deal damage and block attacks. Each card costs energy (3/turn). Beat the employee to win.
THE TAG PHASE
After playing cards, click FILE REPORT. Then TAG up to 3 played cards for your performance report. The suits you tag determine your profit.
MANAGEMENT STYLES
3 different suits = Cross-Functional (x5 profit)
3 same suit = Board Resolution (x8 profit)
2 same suit = Follow-Up (x2 profit)
No match = Ad Hoc (x1 profit)
KEY INSIGHT
Diversity beats pairs! Cross-Functional (x5) is easier AND better than Follow-Up (x2). Tag cards from different suits.
